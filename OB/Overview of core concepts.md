#### study tips
- get a study guide
	- **Saunders comprehensive NCLEX guide**
		- use as a supplement
- remember key concepts that popup consistently
- practice NCLEX style questions
- Maternity nursing is very specialized
	- same type of questions popup regularly
- remember terms that have been named and have acronyms
	- stages of labor
	- mensturual cycle 
	- fetal development through the weeks
	- scoring tools

#### highlights of things that will popup in exams

**Female reproductive system**
- menstrual cycle 
	- phases
- what happens each day (hormones and processes that happen)
- different types of female pelvises
- how the arteries and veins work in the umbilical cord (how many of them)
- function of placenta and how it works
- how and when fertilization and implantation happens
- fetal development week by week

**Assessing the pregnant mother**
- pay attention to specific maternity lingo
	- gravidy
	- parity
	- **GTPAL**
		- refers to the maternal record of the patient
	- **Nagele's rule**
		- used to calculate a woman's due date
	- signs of pregnancy
		- specifically :
			- Chadwick sign
			- Goodell sign
	- risk factors for pregnancy
		- HIV and Hepatitis
		- How the fetus is affected and necessary education in such cases
